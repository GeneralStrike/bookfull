Suggest a book

These book lists are made of suggestions from childless and childfree readers. Your suggestions are vital!

At the moment the book lists are updated by me, the owner of this site, but I need input from you to do it right. If enough people show interest in this project, I plan to make the site more interactive - letting you add, rate and review books yourself.

For the moment, if you've got a book (or many) to suggest, please send me an email with the information below.

  • Title
  • Author
  • ISBN
  • Genre
  • Why we should include it - Let me know if the book is about a childless protagonist, a childfree protagonist, features an infertility journey, is sad or hopeful or just an entertaining story with no kids or pregnancies.
  • Any triggers - Not everyone has trouble reading about the same things. Please consider if any of these are in the book. If there are potential triggers, but you still think the ook is worth including, let me know that too.
    • Miracle Baby
    • Pregnancy
    • Still birth
    • Miscarriage
    • Abortion
    • Adoption
    • Fostering
    • Other parent-child type relationships (found family)
    • Infertility
    • Fertility Treatment
    • Parenting as the meaning of life

Found a typo, missed trigger, or mistake?

I'd love to hear about those too, so please don't hesitate to let me know!
